Team #9 Productions

We named ourselves Team#9 because we’re a team of quizzing enthusiasts who missed out on making the final round of 8 in most quizzes in Bangalore. As the top non-qualifying team, we invariably celebrated by hitting the nearest shady bar. When we did qualify, we’d crack up whenever the quiz hosts said, “The correct answer goes to Team#4, Team#9”.

Season 1 of IWTK marks two years of figuring things out from scratch, research, setting questions, coordinating across four time zones and getting things done. Slowly and steadily.
We received so much love and support from our patient partners, families and friends. We’re grateful beyond words. Some of them even agreed to participate on the show. Nepotism for the win.

With the first episode out now, we’re nervous and excited AF. We hope you enjoy this and help spread the word. That will make us feel like we made it to the final 8.
